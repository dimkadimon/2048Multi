How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Created by Dmitry Kamenetsky based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. This version of 2048 uses blocks of two colours that need to be merged separately. This adds a whole new dimension to the game. Please enjoy and share with your friends!
